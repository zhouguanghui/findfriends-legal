Find Friends Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Find Friends collects, uses, shares, and retains information when you use the Find Friends mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Information You Provide

  • Account information. When you use Sign in with Apple, we receive the Apple user identifier and any name or email address Apple provides for that sign-in. We may also process legacy account credentials when you use a legacy account to sign in.
  • Profile information. You may provide a display name, age, gender, city, bio, tags, profile photo, and Snapchat, Instagram, or Kik usernames.
  • User content. We process posts, post photos, messages, message photos, likes, reports, blocks, and other content or actions you submit through the Service.
  • Support communications. If you contact us, we collect the information you choose to include in your message.

Location Information

With your permission, Find Friends accesses your device's location while you use the app. We use precise location coordinates and a derived city to provide nearby-user features and distance displays. Your city and location may be associated with your profile so that nearby features can work. You can deny or revoke location permission in iOS Settings; nearby features may then be unavailable or less accurate.

Photos and Camera

With your permission, Find Friends accesses the camera or photo library only when you choose to add a profile photo or post photo. The photo you select is uploaded to provide the feature you requested and may be visible to other users according to the context in which you share it.

Device, Notification, and Purchase Information

We and our service providers may process device and app information, such as device identifiers, operating-system information, IP address, app interactions, and push-notification tokens. We use this information to operate the Service, secure accounts, troubleshoot problems, and deliver notifications when you allow them.

If you purchase or restore a VIP subscription, we process the information needed to validate the purchase with Apple, such as an App Store receipt and subscription status. Apple processes payment information; we do not receive your full payment-card number.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create and maintain accounts, profiles, posts, messages, and nearby-user features;
  • connect users with the social usernames they choose to share;
  • process and validate subscriptions, restore purchases, and provide VIP features;
  • protect users, investigate reports, prevent fraud and abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service;
  • send service-related notifications when you have enabled them; and
  • maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the Service.

How Information Is Shared

  • With other users. Information you make public, such as profile information and Square posts, may be visible to other users. Messages are shared with their intended recipients.
  • With service providers. We use service providers to host profile and content data, deliver messaging, provide Apple sign-in and subscription functions, and deliver push notifications. These providers may process information only to provide services to us and as permitted by their own applicable terms.
  • For legal and safety reasons. We may disclose information where reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to valid legal process, protect users or the public, investigate abuse, or enforce our rights.
  • In a business transfer. Information may be transferred as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of assets, or similar transaction, subject to applicable law.

Third-Party Services

Find Friends may open Snapchat, Instagram, Kik, Apple, or other third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy. Find Friends is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Snapchat, Instagram, or Kik.

Data Retention and Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from Settings in the app. We delete or de-identify personal information when it is no longer needed for the purposes described in this Policy, subject to reasonable backup, security, fraud-prevention, legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution requirements. Content copied or shared by another user may remain under that user's control.

Your Choices

  • You can edit profile information in the app.
  • You can control location, camera, photo-library, and notification permissions through iOS Settings.
  • You can block or report other users through the Service.
  • You can request account deletion through Settings in the app.
  • You can manage or cancel Apple subscriptions through your Apple Account subscription settings.

Age Requirement

Find Friends is intended only for people who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe that someone under 18 has provided personal information through the Service, contact us and we will review the report.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after an update becomes effective is subject to the revised policy, to the extent permitted by law.
